Houzi AI (In-App AI Features, introduced in version 1.4.8):
- Model: Bring Your Own Key (BYOK). The site owner supplies their own AI provider API key; it is stored on their WordPress server and is never exposed to the app or end users.
- Supported providers: Google (Gemini), Claude (Anthropic), and OpenAI. Recommended models are the cost-effective flash / lite / mini variants.
- AI Home ("Tailored for You"): an AI-first home screen with a natural-language search box, personalized shortcuts built from the site's own taxonomies (property types, statuses, features), and a "Top Matches for You" row.
- AI Property Search: users type or speak (voice search) what they want in plain language; Houzi AI converts it into structured search filters, returns matching listings, and suggests follow-up refinement chips.
- Voice Search: natural-language property search via on-device speech recognition.
- Ask About This Listing: buyers chat with AI about a specific property; answers are grounded strictly in that listing's data and returned as actionable cards (map directions, agent call/message, floor details).
- Controls: enabled per-feature by the site owner, optional and off by default, with configurable per-user and per-site rate limits, an output-language setting, and location aliases.
- Setup documentation: https://houzi-docs.booleanbites.com/tools/setup_ai_features
