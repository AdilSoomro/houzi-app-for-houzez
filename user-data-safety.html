User Data Safety: Our Commitment to Your Privacy

At Houzi, we prioritize the privacy and security of our users. We understand the significance of safeguarding your personal information and strive to provide a transparent and secure environment. This page outlines our commitment to user data safety, along with instructions on how to delete your account in compliance with Google Play policies.

Why Your Data Safety Matters to Us

For a better experience, while using our Service, we may require you to provide us with certain personally identifiable information, including but not limited to First Name, Last Name, Email, Address, City, Country. The information that we request will be retained by us and used as described in this privacy policy.

The app does use third party services that may collect information used to identify you.

  2. Security Measures:
    Our platform employs robust security measures to prevent unauthorized access, ensuring that your personal information remains confidential.
  3. Compliance with Regulations:
    We adhere to the guidelines and policies set forth by Google Play and other regulatory bodies, prioritizing compliance with industry standards.

Deleting Your Account on Houzi real estate app

Following are the steps that you can use to delete your account on the app.

  1. Step 1: Navigate to Your Profile
    Click on the 'Profile' tab from the home screen bottom section to access your user profile settings.
  2. Step 2: Manage Your Profile
    Within your profile tab, locate the 'Manage Profile' button. Tapping it will open Manager Profile section.
  3. Step 3: Delete My Account:
    In the Manager Profile screen, press the red button titled 'Delete My Account' to initiate the account deletion process.
You can at any time delete these information about you on our website and mobile app. The screenshot below shows how you can access these options.

Our Commitment to Google Play Policies

We understand the importance of complying with Google Play policies to ensure a safe and reliable experience for all users. Our practices align with Google Play's guidelines on user data, security, and privacy.

  1. Data Minimization:
    We only collect the data necessary for the functionality of our app, minimizing the collection of personal information.
  2. Secure Data Transmission:
    All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted, ensuring secure communication.
  3. Account Deletion:
    Our 'Delete My Account' feature enables users to permanently delete their profiles and related data, as per Google Play policies.
